conf.kde.in '15

As with the previous editions, conf.kde.in is a platform for Qt/KDE contributors, developers and enthusiasts to come together, share their knowledge, contribute, learn and play. Most importantly it's an excellent arena for collaboration and exchange of ideas, skills and thoughts among like minded people passionate about open source in general.

conf.kde.in is an awesome opportunity for any student who is passionate to experience the magic of open source. Here you can meet the veterans in open source software development, develop professional connections which , for sure, will help you a great deal in future, chat with student contributors who can guide you , present in front of a grand audience (wow!) and most importantly grasp the essence of open source development. It is indeed a ‘never miss’ opportunity that will give you a strong foundation to build on!

Welcome to CONF.KDE.IN

KDE conferences in India, organized by KDE-India go by the name conf.kde.in (abbreviated as cKi) and have usually attract high quality participants and speakers alike. It was first held in 2011, as a 5 day event in Bangalore, attracting around 300 participants and turned out be a huge success and it introduced a lot of people to Open Source and KDE and the 2015 edition aims to take this forward. The last offering of the event was one of the largest event of this kind to be held in the country, with close to 400 participants attending the mega event.

This year, the conf.kde.in makes it's foray into the southern ends of the country. The 2015 edition of the event would be held in Amritapuri - located in the picturesque village of Vallikavu, in the district of Kollam, Kerala. Amritapuri is the spiritual headquarter of world renowned humanitarian and spiritual leader Mata Amritanandamayi. Her university, Amrita University, however is also famous for the FOSS culture it has garnered. The FOSS activities carried out in the club have helped students of the university many laurels, some of which can be found here : http://foss.amrita.ac.in/achievement/viewall/

KDE is one of the largest open source communities in the world. It has received the love and care of several world renowned developers who have made it what it is today and continues to be actively developed to this date.

Through this event, it is hoped that speakers, delegates and students of host and nearby educational institutions take this grand opportunity to talk, share, learn, discuss, debate and do software development. If you are interested to help organize the conf.kde.in 2014, please don't hesitate to contact us
